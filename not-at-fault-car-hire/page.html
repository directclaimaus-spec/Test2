Not at Fault? You’re Eligible for a Free Accident Replacement Car

If you’ve been in a not at fault car accident, you’re legally entitled to a replacement hire car — even if you don’t have insurance. At Direct Claims Melbourne, we’ll deliver your hire vehicle the same day while your damaged car is being assessed or repaired.
We handle the entire process for you, including communication with the at-fault driver’s insurer, so you pay nothing out of pocket. Once your claim is approved, we recover all hire costs directly from the other party’s insurer.
Whether you drive a compact car, SUV, or prestige vehicle, we’ll match your vehicle type and keep you mobile until yours is back on the road — fast, fair, and completely stress-free.

Why Choose Not at Fault Car Hire Instead of Going Through Your Insurer?

You don’t need to lodge a claim through your insurance company to access a Not at Fault Hire Car. Whether you’re insured or uninsured, you have the legal right to a replacement vehicle at no cost when another party is at fault.

At Direct Claims Melbourne, we handle everything directly with the at-fault driver’s insurer, saving you the time, frustration, and potential premium increases that can come from claiming through your own policy.

Our dedicated team ensures same-day vehicle delivery, genuine support, and transparent communication — making your accident management experience fast and stress-free.
